Wardrobe Optimizer™

Have you ever felt like you wear the same combination of clothes to work/school?

Let us help you! Here's how it works:

Input the clothes you plan on wearing into the "Add New" button.

Everyday before you leave (or after you return), input the clothes that you wore that day. You can see the fits you wore on the "Calendar" Button.

Overtime, the program will pick up on combinations that you have and have not worn. You will be able to select the "Random" button once a day and it will give you a combination that the program is not seen you wear.

You can add new clothes to the program at any point, giving you more combination varients! When viewing your clothes, the program will also tell you how many times you've worn a specific item and the last time that you wore it
